Professional Profile

Letsebela Lebenya has extensive experience in Property Development and Management, Real Estate, ICT, Electronics and Mathematics. ICT has been her primary focus area over the past 20 years. She is currently a Principal Property Practitioner at her own Real Estate Agency, Property Developer and an Independent ICT and Engineering Consultant.

Her previous ICT related positions included Senior Manager: EA, BA and PMO in the largest Water Utility company in Africa, Enterprise Architect and IRD Lead, Head of ICT, Systems Analyst Lead, Business Process analyst, and ICT strategy specialist. She has also held positions as project manager, electronics engineer and researcher earlier in her career.

She is currently studying towards a PhD in Computer Engineering, where her topic is "Knowledge Transfer for Developmental Learning of Robot Kinematics". She holds a Masters' Degree in IT, a BSc Honours in Electronics, and a BSc in Electronics and Mathematics.
